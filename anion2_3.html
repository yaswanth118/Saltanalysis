Conformation of Iodide ion.

As per your observation the possible anion might be iodide ion.
But we need to do conformation test for this ion.Now follow the given steps in order to confirm the anion present.
  • Take 1 mL of salt solution neutralised with HCl and add 1 mL chloroform/carbon tetrachloride/carbon disulphide. Now add an excess of chlorine water drop wise and shake the test tube. A violet colour appears in the organic layer.
  • Take 1 mL of sodium carbonate extract acidify it with dil. HNO 3 (or take water extract). Add, silver nitrate solution. A yellow precipitate insoluble in NH 4 OH solution is obtained.
