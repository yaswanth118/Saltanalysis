Chemistry behind this analysis

  1. Add conc. HNO 3 and ammonium molybdate solution to the test solution containing phosphate ions and boil. A yellow colouration in solution or a canary yellow precipitate of ammonium-phosphomolybdate, (NH4)3[P(Mo3O10)4] is formed. Each oxygen of phosphate has been replaced by Mo3O10 group.

    • Na2HPO4 + 12 (NH4)2MoO4 + 23 HNO3 ---> (NH4)3[P(Mo3O10)4] (Canary Yellow ppt) + 2 NaNO3 + 21 NH4NO3 + 12 H2O
