Conformation of Nitrite ion.

As per your observation the possible anion might be nitrite ion.
But we need to do conformation test for this ion.Now follow the given steps in order to confirm the anion present.
  • Take 1 mL of water extract in a test tube. Add a fewdrops of potassium iodide solution and a few drops ofstarch solution, acidify with acetic acid. Blue colour appears.
  • Acidify 1 mL of water extract with acetic acid. Add 2-3 drops of sulphanilic acid solution followed by 2-3 drops of 1-naphthylamine reagent. Appearance of redcolour indicates the presence of nitrite ion.


Perform the conformation tests given above.
