Conformation of Sulphate ion.

Now follow the given steps in order to confirm the anion present.
  • Take 1 mL water extract of the salt in water or sodium carbonate and after acidifying with dilute hydrochloric acid add BaCl2 solution. White precipitate insoluble in conc. HCl or conc. HNO3 is obtained.
  • Acidify the aqueous solution or sodium carbonate extract with acetic acid and add lead acetate solution. Appearance of white precipitate confirms the presence of sulphate ion.


Perform the two conformation tests given above.
