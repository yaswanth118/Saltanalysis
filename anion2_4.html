Conformation of Nitrate ion.

As per your observation the possible anion might be nitrate ion.
But we need to do conformation test for this ion.Now follow the given steps in order to confirm the anion present.
  • Take 1 mL of salt solution in water in a test tube. Add 2 mL conc. of H2SO4 and mix thoroughly. Cool the mixture under the tap. Add freshly prepared ferrous sulphate along the sides of the test tube without shaking. A dark brown ring is formed at the junction of the two solutions.


Perform the conformation test given above.
